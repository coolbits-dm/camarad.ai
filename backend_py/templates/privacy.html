2. What data we process

  • Account data: identifiers needed for authentication and account management (e.g., email address, user ID, basic profile fields).
  • Workspace/client context: workspace membership, active client selection, settings and preferences.
  • Chat and run data: messages, prompts, generated outputs, traces, workflow execution metadata.
  • Connector data: metadata and reporting data retrieved from connected services (e.g., Google Ads / GA4) within the permissions you grant.
  • Technical data: logs and security events (IP address, timestamps, request metadata) needed to operate and protect the Service.

3. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR)

  • Provide the Service (account, sessions, workspaces, runs) - performance of a contract.
  • Security and abuse prevention - legitimate interests.
  • Connector operations (fetching data from connected services) - performance of a contract and your instructions.
  • Support and communications - legitimate interests; in some cases your consent.

4. OAuth and connected Google services

If you sign in with Google or connect Google services (e.g., Ads/GA4), we process OAuth tokens and authorized data strictly to provide connector functionality. You can revoke access from your Google account settings and/or disconnect within the Service where available.
